Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to any person who provides information as part of the registration or reporting process. By using the BALIK system, the registrant also consents to the University/Organization to collect, use, process, and access any personal information appertaining to any purposes deemed legal. All information provided by the registrant will be treated with full confidentiality.

Collection of User Data

We collect personal information from students, faculty, staff, visitors, and externals for the purpose of facilitating our lost and found management services.

  • Full name
  • Age and Affiliation (Student, Faculty, Visitor, etc.)
  • Contact information (Email address)
  • Item details (Descriptions and photographs of lost or found items)
  • Identification records (For verification during item claims)

Use of User Data

We use the data collected through the BALIK system solely for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating item recovery services, matching lost reports with found items, and coordinating communication between finders and owners.
  • Verifying ownership during the claim process to ensure items are returned to the correct individuals.
  • Providing technical support and maintenance for the BALIK management platform.
  • Conducting statistical analysis to improve system efficiency, provided that the data is anonymized and aggregated.

Terms of Service

By accessing or using the BALIK platform, you signify your agreement to be bound by these Terms of Service, which govern your conduct and responsibilities while reporting or claiming lost and found items within the system.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the BALIK platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use the system.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Accuracy of Information: You agree to provide true, accurate, and complete information when registering an account or filing a report (Lost or Found).
  • Honesty in Claims: Users must not filed fraudulent claims. Claiming an item that does not belong to you is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.
  • Verification: You agree to cooperate with the system administrators or security personnel during the verification process, which may include providing proof of ownership or valid identification.

3. Prohibited Conduct

  • Users shall not use the platform to harass, threaten, or defraud other users.
  • You may not upload false images or descriptions of items to mislead the community or administrators.

4. Limitation of Liability

The BALIK system is a facilitation tool. While we strive to ensure the safe return of items, the platform and its administrators are not responsible for the physical condition of found items or for items that are not successfully recovered.
